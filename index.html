I'm Gerrit-Jan

Great to have you here at my website! I work on climate and air pollution research, with a special interest to the data analysis, modelling, and software engineering. While the variety of topics I work on at TNO is quite broad within the atmospheric science domain, a recurring theme is how to make a change right here and right now. Interested? Look for the projects I am currently working on and feel free to reach out!
