Network analysis can be used to arrive at smart inspections. That is the main outcome of my PhD dissertation entitled: "Network Analysis Methods for Smart Inspection in the Transport Domain". On November 16th 2023 I defended my thesis, wherein we arrive at a data-driven approach towards selection vehicles that should be inspected.

The research was carried out together with the Human Environment and Transport Inspectorate and the Dutch Ministry of Infrastructure and Water Management.
