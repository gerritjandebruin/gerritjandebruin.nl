TNO is partner in the Ruisdael Observatory, where we contribute with our expertise in atmospheric measurements and modelling. The Ruisdael Observatory is a nationalwide observatory for measurements of the atmosphere. We provide our Cabauw measurements and measurements from our mobile van and aerosol trailer to the Ruisdael Observatory.

My role is to clean the data and make it easily accessible available to the Ruisdael Observatory.
